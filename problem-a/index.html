Rei Furuya

Introduction

Rei Furuya is a character in the manga: Detective Conan. Ostensibly works as a private detective and part-time waiter at a cafe. Under the pseudonym Amuro Tooru.

His real identity is an undercover agent sent by the Japanese Public Security Police Agency into a black criminal organization. Code name Bourbon.
